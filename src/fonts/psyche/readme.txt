Psyche (or ψυχή - “soul” from Greek) is display typeface that excels in music covers, posters, impactful headlines, fashion design and more.

The name Psyche, as well as the style of the typeface, combines two trends, Greek culture and underground culture, rave music and chaos. The word Psyche (ψυχή) was taken from the Greek language, its spelling is reminiscent of the word psycho, those who people can become at rave parties and loud concerts). The word translates as soul or spirit, a place where music has a huge influence.

Psyche is well suited for impactful headlines, stylized posters and bright artwork.

The font is free.
